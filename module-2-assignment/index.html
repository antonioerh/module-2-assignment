Types of Meat

Beef

Beef is the meat from cattle and is one of the most widely consumed meats around the world. It is rich in protein, iron, and other nutrients. Common cuts include steak, ribs, brisket, and ground beef. Beef can be grilled, roasted, or stewed, and is often used in burgers, steaks, and traditional dishes.

Pork

Pork comes from pigs and is known for its tenderness and flavor. It includes cuts like pork chops, ribs, bacon, and ham. Pork is very versatile and can be cooked in many ways, such as roasting, frying, or barbecuing. It's commonly found in dishes from many cultures worldwide.

Chicken

Chicken is a popular white meat known for being lean, mild in flavor, and easy to cook. It includes parts like the breast, thigh, wings, and drumsticks. Chicken is used in countless recipes, from grilled dishes to soups and stir-fries, and is a staple protein in many diets.
